Kelly’s Food Pantry is the central El Paso food distribution center for the El Pasoans Fighting Hunger Food Bank. We’re proud to receive the majority of our food pantry’s resources each day from the food bank, and are extremely grateful for their partnership in fighting hunger in our community.
