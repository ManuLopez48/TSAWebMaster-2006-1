Kelly’s Food Pantry is the central El Paso food distribution center for the El Pasoans Fighting Hunger Food Bank. We’re proud to receive the majority of our food pantry’s resources each day from the food bank, and are extremely grateful for their partnership in fighting hunger in our community.

When you visit the pantry we will request your: name, address, # of individuals in household, and monthly household income. Cuando visite la despensa, le solicitaremos su: nombre, dirección, número de personas en el hogar e ingreso familiar mensual. We recommend you bring a form of identification when visiting the pantry – and a cart or box to take home the food you receive!
