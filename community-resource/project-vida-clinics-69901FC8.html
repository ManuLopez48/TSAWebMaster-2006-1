Project Vida Health Center clinics support the health and well-being of patients in all stages of life, for all ages. We offer a full range of primary health care services at multiple locations throughout El Paso and Hudspeth Counties. We are patient-centered and believe the patient’s health is the primary concern when planning and operating all Project Vida Health Center services and supporting programs.

Clinic services include, but are not limited to the following:

Wellness Check-ups

Immunizations

Labs

Telehealth

Pharmacy

Diabetes and Hypertension Diagnosis, Care, and Follow-up

Pre- and Post-natal Care

Women’s Health Care

Pediatrics

Well-Child Check-ups

Family Planning

Acute and Chronic Conditions

HIV, HepC, PreP, PEP

STI

Counseling and Integrated Behavioral Health Care
